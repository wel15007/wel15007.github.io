Target Audience Description

This website is target to those interested in what the weather is going to be for the day and throughout the week.

Persona #1: Commercial Driver

One person who might use this website would be a commercial licenced driver who relys on good road and weather conditions and might want to look at what the weather is going to be like today and if he needs to prepare for good weather and good road conditions or if the weather is bad that day and needs to prepeare for bad weather and road conditions.

Persona #2: Outdoor event planner

Another person who might use this website is an outdoor event planner. There might be an event that someone is planning for and they need to see what the weather is like in their location and if they need to bring additional equipment for specific types of weather, like a cannopy if it is rainy or sunny, a sweater or jacket if it is cold, or sunscreen if it is hot and sunny.

Scenarios

Here are five scenarios that our site will be able to answer:

  • What is the weather going to be like for the week?
  • I am planning on having a picnic today, will it rain today?
  • I want to go to a sporting event today, is it going to be cold outside today?
  • I am driving on the road today, is it snowing?
  • I plan on being outside for most of the day, is it going to be cold today? Do I need to wear a coat or just a jacket?
