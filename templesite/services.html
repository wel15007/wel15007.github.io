Amenities

Here are some of the free amenities that Temples Inns and Suites provides:

  • Breakfast
  • An Exercise/workout room
  • An Indoor Pool
  • Wi-Fi high-speed internet access
  • Smart TV's
  • Parking
  • Temple Shuttle
  • Family history consultation
  • Family ordinance card services

Specialized Services

Here are some specialized services that we provide:

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes

  • A playroom with low-cost, short term babysitting services for the children of patrons.

  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area.

  • A family search center that supports family search and ordinance work.
